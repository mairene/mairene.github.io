As an alternative to static selfie photos, Yago is a JavaScript-based app that allows users to connects to a Parrot AR.Drone 2.0, control it via the keyboard, and record videos into a local folder. It uses sockets.io, an express.js framework, homebrew, and jQuery to communicate with and retrieve information from the drone such as GPS navigation data and video-feed images. Built in one week. Team members: Rafa Auriermo, Dillon Downey, Dan Klos, Irene Ma, Grace Yasakawa
